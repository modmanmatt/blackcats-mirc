 Installation Guide :

 

 

 Unzip all the files of the .zip to your mIRC directory, keeping the "Use folder names" option enabled.

 Type //load -rs " $+ $mircdirg5x-poker\g5x-poker.mrc $+ "  or  use your mIRC Editor to browse and load the script.

 

 

 

 Game Info :

 

 

 Important Notes :-

 * Requirements : mIRC 6.03 or above.

 * Game performance : Disabling the game sounds greatly speeds up the game performance!

 

 How to play :

  Type /g5x-poker to start the game.

  * More options are available through the drop down menu on right-click...

 

  1. Press the "DEAL Now!" button to start a new deal, a new series of cards will be displayed.

  2. You may now drop some bad cards if there are any, this can be done by a single click on the desired card(s),

      then/else press the "DEAL Now!" button again.

  3. You will be rewarded according to the combination of cards you get.

  4. Restart again... for a new deal!

 

  Grade Rewards are as follows :

  grade 1 » $ 2000  (if the strip poker mode is enabled, you should get your picture no.1)

  grade 2 » $ 4000

  grade 3 » $ 7000

  grade 4 » $ 10000

 

  Info on the Strip Poker mode :

  Strip Poker mode must be turned ON, so as to enable the "Check for New Girls" button of the Game Options.

  Then select your girl and Deal again! you shall be rewarded.

 

  Info on the Keyboard Shortcuts keys :

  Esc »  minimizes the game's window, this might be useful  ;-)

  n    »  starts a new game.

  c    »  closes the game.

  o    »  opens the game options dialog.

  p    »  opens the picture manager dialog.

  a    »  switches the strip poker mode to on.

  z    »  switches the strip poker mode to off.

  e    »  enables all the game sounds.

  d    »  disables all the game sounds.

  w   »  enables/disables the win sound.

  f    »   enables/disables the card flip sound.

 Poker Rules Info :

 

 

  Royal Flush

Ace, king, queen, jack and 10 all of the same suit. The highest possible straight flush.

 

       

 

  Straight Flush

Five cards of the same suit in sequence.

 

       

 

  Four of a Kind

Four cards of the same rank and any other card.

 

       

 

  Full House

Three cards of one rank and two cards of another rank.

 

       

 

  Flush

Any of five cards of the same suit.

 

        

 

  Straight

Any five cards in sequence.

 

       

 

  Three of a Kind

Three cards of same rank and two unmatched cards.

 

       

 

  Two pairs

Two cards of one rank, two cards of second rank, and an unmatched fifth card.

 

       

 

  Jacks or better

One pair of Jacks or Queens or Kings or Aces.
